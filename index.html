FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
What industries do you serve with your raw materials and industrial machinery?

Ermes Trading Limited serves a wide range of industries, including automotive, construction, food and beverage, healthcare, and manufacturing. Our raw materials and industrial machinery are designed to meet the specific needs of each industry, ensuring high performance and reliability.

How can I place an order for products on your website?

Placing an order with Ermes Trading Limited is simple. First, browse our product catalog to find the items you need. Once you have selected your products, send a request through our website or contact our sales team directly. We will confirm your order details and provide you with the necessary information to complete the purchase.

Do you offer customization services for industrial machinery?

Yes, we do offer customization services for our industrial machinery. Our team of experienced engineers can work with you to tailor the machinery to your specific requirements. Please contact us with your customization needs, and we will provide you with a detailed proposal and timeline.

What are your shipping and delivery policies?

Ermes Trading Limited provides international shipping for all our products. We partner with reliable logistics companies to ensure timely and safe delivery. Shipping costs and delivery times vary based on the destination and the size of the order. Once your order is confirmed, we will provide you with tracking information and an estimated delivery date.
